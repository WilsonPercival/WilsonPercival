## Problem description

I have already reported this: https://github.com/Scirra/Construct-3-bugs/issues/6643

When I hit `Undo` in `Ease editor`, the yellow dot doesn't come back.

## Attach a .c3p

[ease_editor_yellow_point_undo_after_fixed_bug_r333b.zip](https://github.com/WilsonPercival/WilsonPercival/files/10919138/ease_editor_yellow_point_undo_after_fixed_bug_r333b.zip)

## Steps to reproduce

1. Open a project.
2. Open `Ease editor`.
3. If you have problems with the camera - lower it lower so that it does not fly up when creating a yellow dot.
4. Create a new yellow dot.
5. Move the yellow dot.
6. Click `Toggle`.
7. Click `Undo`.
8. Click `Undo`. Notice that nothing happened.
9. Click `Undo`. Notice that nothing happened.

## Expected result

When I press `Undo` in `Ease editor` the yellow dot comes back.
